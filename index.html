How to Play

Try and find the character of the day! Start by guessing any character on the list, then use the hints to guess more characters until you find the right answer.

A gray tile means the answer is completely wrong, a yellow tile (with the flame of Tar Valon in the background) is partially correct, and a green tile (with the original ancient symbol of the Aes Sedai) is fully correct.

Yellow tiles might include a number at the bottom right corner. This indicates the number of correct matches. For example, if you guessed Rand, who is a Human, Male, Andoran, and there is a 2 in the corner, the correct character could be a Human, Female, Andoran or maybe a Human, Male, Shienaran.

The "Born" column shows the year in the New Era (NE) the character was born. For reference, the main books take place from NE 998 to 1000. If there is a down arrow, the correct character was born in an earlier year, and vice versa for an up arrow. If there is no arrow, the correct character's birth year is Unknown.
